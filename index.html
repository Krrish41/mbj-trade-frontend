About MBJ Global

Built for high-trust, large-volume agro trade.

MBJ Global Pvt. Ltd. is the global trading arm of Shantiratan Empire, a prominent Indian agro-industry group established in 2007.

We specialize in domestic and international trading of premium wheat, flour products, pulses, rice, sugar, and agro commodities. Backward integration through large-scale flour mills and processing units helps us deliver consistent quality, competitive pricing, and dependable supply.

Our vision is to bridge the pure essence of Indian agriculture with international markets while promoting sustainability, quality, and food security.
